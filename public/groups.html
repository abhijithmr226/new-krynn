World Cup 2026 Group Stage Format

The expanded tournament consists of 48 nations split into 12 groups of 4 teams. Check out the qualification rules and layout.

Progression & Qualification Rules

To qualify for the **Round of 32**, teams must secure points during the group stage. The points are awarded as follows: 3 for a win, 1 for a draw, and 0 for a loss.

Who Qualifies:

  • Group Winners (12 Teams): Automatically qualify.
  • Group Runners-Up (12 Teams): Automatically qualify.
  • Best 3rd-Place Teams (8 Teams): The 8 best-performing third-place teams across all 12 groups will qualify based on goal difference, goals scored, and fair play points.

This layout ensures that almost every final group matchday remains highly competitive, as third-place teams seek to maximize their statistics to secure knockout phase tickets.
