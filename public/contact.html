Frequently Asked Questions
  • The stream is buffering or not playing. What do I do?
    Try switching the stream format (HLS ↔ TS) using the format selector in the player header. The platform will also auto-switch formats if a stream is detected as unavailable. If problems persist, try a different feed from the Feeds menu.
  • Why is a channel showing offline?
    Channels are periodically health-checked. If a channel appears offline, it may be temporarily down on the provider's end. You can run a fresh health check using the Check Channels button in the Live TV panel.
  • How do I add my own IPTV portal?
    KRYNN SPORTS supports Xtream Codes API and M3U playlist portals. Edit the data/portals.json config file on the server to add your credentials.
  • Is KRYNN SPORTS free?
    Yes. The web interface is completely free. You need a valid IPTV subscription or M3U playlist from a provider to access content streams.
  • How do I report a broken stream or content issue?
    Please send us a DM on Instagram @krynnlabs with the channel name and the issue you're experiencing.
  • I have a DMCA or legal request. Who do I contact?
    Contact us via Instagram DM. DMCA and legal requests are handled within 72 hours of receipt. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
Stream Issues During World Cup
High-traffic match days (especially group stage knockout matches) may cause provider-side instability. If your primary feed is down, use the Feeds Menu inside the player to instantly switch to an alternate broadcast. The platform also auto-detects stalled streams and switches format automatically.
