Expanded Format Confirms 104 Total Matches

The FIFA council approved a 4-team group structure resulting in 104 matches, extending the tournament length to 39 days. Fans will enjoy up to 6 matches daily during peak group stage action.

Published: June 2026

Estadio Azteca Prepares for Historic Opening Match

Estadio Azteca in Mexico City will become the first stadium to host matches in three separate FIFA World Cups. Renovation works on locker rooms and pitch drainage systems are fully completed.

Published: June 2026

Travel Distance Mitigation Guidelines Enforced

To reduce team fatigue, matches are grouped into regional zones (East, Central, West). Teams will complete their group stage fixtures within their assigned zone to limit transcontinental flights.
