Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

KRYNN SPORTS is a browser-based live streaming portal. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics such as page views, session duration, and general geographic region (country level) collected via standard server logs.
  • Local Storage: Your channel favorites, player preferences, and UI settings are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • IP Address: Your IP address may be logged by our server for security, abuse prevention, and diagnostic purposes. It is not sold or shared with third parties.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to save:

  • Your favorited channels, movies, and series
  • Your selected stream format preferences (HLS/TS)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookie networks. Some embedded CDN resources (e.g., fonts from Google Fonts) may set their own cookies subject to Google's own privacy policy.

3. Third-Party Services

KRYNN SPORTS uses the following third-party resources whose own privacy policies apply:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Inter, Outfit). Google may log font requests.
  • Font Awesome CDN — for icons. Cloudflare may log requests.
  • Shaka Player / HLS.js / mpegts.js — open source video player libraries loaded from CDN.
  • StreamScore / Sportradar — live match data API for scoreboard functionality.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other behavioural tracking services.

4. Stream Content & IPTV

KRYNN SPORTS acts as a client interface for IPTV streams. Stream URLs and channel metadata are fetched from IPTV provider APIs configured by the operator of this instance. We are not responsible for the content provided by those third-party IPTV sources.

Your IPTV portal credentials (if any) are stored only in server-side configuration files on the host machine and are never transmitted to any analytics or advertising service.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the data processed by this service:

  • All stream proxying is performed server-side to prevent direct credential exposure in the browser.
  • Server logs are periodically purged and are not accessible publicly.
  • We recommend accessing this service over HTTPS to protect data in transit.
6. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal personal data, your primary rights are:

  • Right to clear local data: You can clear all locally stored preferences by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site at any time.
  • Right to request deletion: If you believe we hold any personal data about you (e.g., from server logs), you may contact us to request deletion.
  • Right to know: You can always contact us to ask what data, if any, is associated with your IP address in our logs.
7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please reach out via our Contact page or follow us on Instagram @krynnlabs.

We will respond to all legitimate privacy requests within 30 days.
