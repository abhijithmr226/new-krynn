How We Calculate Probabilities

Our forecasting system calculates the win, draw, and loss chances for each fixture by running millions of simulations using three primary statistical vectors:

  1. FIFA & Elo Ratings: Analyzes historical strength rankings, adjusted for recent competitive matches and quality of opponents.
  2. Team Form & Fatigue: Evaluates same-stage performance over the last 10 fixtures, rest intervals, and travel distances between host venues.
  3. Roster & Injury Factors: Dynamically adjusts ratings based on starting lineups, suspensions, and primary goal-scorer availability.

These calculations update dynamically right up to kickoff when official starting lineups are submitted.
